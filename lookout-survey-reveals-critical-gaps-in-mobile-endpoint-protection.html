Lookout Survey Reveals Critical Gaps in Mobile Endpoint Protection That Could Compromise Sensitive Corporate Cloud Data


Lookout Survey Reveals Critical Gaps in Mobile Endpoint Protection That Could Compromise Sensitive Corporate Cloud Data Our survey of cybersecurity leaders in the U.S. underscores not only the growing danger of mobile phishing attacks and why (and how quickly) they could result in the loss of sensitive corporate data, but also the gaps that many companies still have in their mobile endpoint security strategies to combat against the modern threat landscape.


97% see mobile apps with extensive permissions as a possible security risk, and 75% encountered phishing attempts targeting their employees in the past six months.

For those on the frontline, this is a stark wake-up call - in the digital world, every second counts.
